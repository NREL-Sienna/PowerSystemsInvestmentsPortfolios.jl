
## Overview

`PowerSystemsInvestmentsPortfolios.jl` is a [`Julia`](http://www.julialang.org) package that provides data models and structures for representing power systems investment portfolios, including technologies, regions, financial data, and investment requirements.

* * *

PowerSystemsInvestmentsPortfolios has been developed as part of the NREL Sienna initiative at the U.S. Department of Energy's National Renewable Energy Laboratory ([NREL](https://www.nrel.gov/)).
